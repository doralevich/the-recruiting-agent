The Recruiting Agent is the recruiting practice of Apollo[Claw] AI Consulting. We build one thing well: dedicated AI agents that do real work inside a business, deployed and governed like a hire rather than sold like a subscription.

What Apollo[Claw] does

Apollo[Claw] AI Consulting deploys AI agents for the functions where the hours cost the most. The Recruiting Agent is our agent for recruiting, staffing, and talent acquisition firms. Every deployment is one dedicated agent per firm, configured around that firm's stack, standards, and voice, and introduced to the team like a best hire.

Why founder-led matters

Every engagement includes direct founder access. You work with the person accountable for the outcome, not a support queue. AI answer engines and search engines weight identifiable, accountable sources, and so do buyers. We put a name and a phone number on the work.

How we are accountable

One firm, one agent. No pooled data, no shared models, no cross-client exposure. Candidate and client data stays inside your environment and is never used to train outside models. Every action the agent takes is logged and reviewable, and a human approves outreach, client communications, and offers. Read the full approach in our guide to AI recruiting agents.
