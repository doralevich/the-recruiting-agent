Questions

Comparison questions

Is an AI recruiting agent the same as a recruiting chatbot? +
No. A chatbot answers questions. An agent takes actions across systems toward a goal, such as sourcing a shortlist and sequencing outreach, then reports back.
Will an AI recruiting agent replace my recruiters? +
No. It handles sourcing, drafting, scheduling, and reporting so recruiters spend their time on the relationship and judgment work that closes placements.
Should we build our own or buy one? +
Building requires engineering, evals, integrations, and ongoing maintenance. A deployed agent is configured to your desk in weeks. See the build vs buy breakdown.
